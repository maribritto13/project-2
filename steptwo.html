Step 2:

Prepare the Pasta Ingredients

1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. 2. Insert your bow-tie pasta and cook for 9-10 minutes. 3. On a cutting board, chop the onions into tiny pieces. 4. Mince the garlic.

Tip:

To prevent sticking and add some flavor, mix in some olive oil and salt to your pasta water!
