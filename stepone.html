Step 1:

Prepare the Butternut Squash

1. Peel and cut the butternut squash into tiny cubes.

2. Add the squash to a baking sheet with a large drizzle of olive oil.

3. Season the cubes with some salt, onion powder, garlic powder, and black pepper.

4. Bake on 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Tip:

Flip your squash after 15 minutes for a more even bake!
