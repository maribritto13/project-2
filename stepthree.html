Step 3:

Cook and Combine

1. Drizzle olive oil in a pan. 2. Add the onions, garlic, and sage. 3. Saute for 1-2 minutes, then add your Italian sausage, mixing until browned. 4. Add the spinach, squash, and cooked pasta to the pan. 5. Top it off with some olive oil, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper to taste.

Tip:

Add some red pepper flakes and paprika for extra flavor!
