Ingredients:

1/2 cup of finely chopped onions 3 minced cloves of garlic 2 tsp of powdered sage 1 cup of butternut squash 1/2 lb Italian sausage 2 cups of bow-tie pasta 3 cups of fresh spinach 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan extra virgin olive oil

Tip:

This recipe makes 2 servings. If you would like to make more, just double all of the ingredients quantities!
